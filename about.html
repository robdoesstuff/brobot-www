About Brobot

Frustrated by low product launch availability being taken up mostly by scalpers, Brobot was originally written as a simple notification bot to allow for a small group of people to simply stop having to refresh web pages constantly to watch for restocks. Since then, it has grown in sophistication and has developed into a dedicated community of PC and gaming hardware enthusiasts.


Brobot is a software service running in the cloud, and by cloud, I mean it's this heap of parts on a temporary folding table
